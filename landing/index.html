Features

Everything you need to study smarter

One extension. Zero extra setup. Just install, capture, and ask.

📸
Screenshot Capture
Capture any tab instantly. Ask the AI to explain diagrams, slides, PDFs, or anything on your screen.
🎙️
Audio Transcription
Record your lecture or voice note and get an instant transcript powered by Whisper — then ask questions about it.
💬
AI Chat
Multi-turn conversations with Llama 4. Attach files, images, and code — all in one thread with full history.
📝
Smart Notes
Save any AI response as a note. Browse, search, and revisit your knowledge base from the built-in dashboard.
🃏
Flashcard Mode
Ask LookUp to turn any topic into interactive flashcards. Flip, review, and test yourself — all in-browser.
🧠
Quiz Generation
One click to convert any material into a quiz with reveal-on-demand answers. Perfect for exam prep.
How it works

Three steps to your AI tutor

No account. No server. Just load unpacked in Chrome and you're ready.

1

Install the extension

Download the zip from GitHub, unpack it, and load it in Chrome — takes under a minute. Enter your free Groq API key and you're live.

2

Capture your content

Screenshot any tab, paste an image, drop a file, or record your voice. LookUp understands text, visuals, and audio equally.

3

Ask anything

Type your question. Get a clear explanation, a quiz, flashcards, or a structured summary — instantly, using state-of-the-art AI.
