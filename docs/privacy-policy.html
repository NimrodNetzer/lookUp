LookUp — Study Sensei

Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: March 2026

Short version: LookUp sends your screenshots, audio, and text only to Groq (for AI processing) — only when you explicitly trigger it. Nothing is collected passively. No data is sent to any server we operate. All your notes and conversations are stored locally on your device.

1. What data LookUp processes

LookUp processes the following types of data, only when you take an explicit action (clicking a button or sending a message):

  • Screenshots — a PNG image of your active browser tab, captured when you press the screenshot button.
  • Audio — audio from your current tab, recorded when you press the record button. Used for transcription only.
  • Text selections — text you have highlighted on a page, sent as context when you ask a question.
  • Chat messages — the messages you type in the LookUp chat interface.

2. Where your data goes

All AI processing is performed by Groq (groq.com). Screenshots, audio, text, and chat messages are sent directly from your browser to api.groq.com using your personal Groq API key.

  • LookUp does not operate any server that receives your data.
  • LookUp does not collect analytics, telemetry, or usage statistics.
  • Groq's own privacy policy governs how Groq handles data sent to their API. See groq.com/privacy-policy.

3. What is stored locally on your device

The following data is stored entirely on your device using Chrome's IndexedDB and chrome.storage.local:

  • Your Groq API key
  • Conversation and message history
  • Notes you have saved
  • Folders and organisation preferences

This data never leaves your device except as part of API requests to Groq described above. You can delete all stored data at any time by clearing the extension's storage from the Chrome extension settings page (chrome://extensions → Details → Clear data).

4. Permissions used and why

  • activeTab / tabs — to capture a screenshot of your current tab and to open the dashboard/chat pages.
  • tabCapture — to capture tab audio for on-demand voice transcription.
  • storage — to save your API key and preferences locally.
  • sidePanel — to display the LookUp assistant panel.
  • Content script on all URLs — to detect text you have selected on any page so you can send it as context to the AI. The script runs passively and does not transmit any data unless you take an action.

5. Third-party services

LookUp uses Groq API exclusively for AI features. No other third-party services, SDKs, or analytics libraries are included.

6. Children's privacy

LookUp is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

7. Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will change. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Open an issue on the project repository or contact us at the email listed on the Chrome Web Store listing.
